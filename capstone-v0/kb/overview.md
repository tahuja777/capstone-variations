# NovaCRM Overview
NovaCRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform designed for small and medium enterprises.
It offers modular components such as Billing, Campaigns, and Support, enabling businesses to manage customer interactions, payments, and communications efficiently.

Key Highlights:
- REST and GraphQL APIs for developers.
- Built-in automation and workflow triggers.
- Data hosted in ISO-27001 certified data centers.
- Tiered plans with flexible billing cycles (monthly or annual).
